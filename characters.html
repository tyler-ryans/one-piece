Jurassic Park (1993) — Dinosaurs & People
Tyrannosaurus rex dinosaur
Breakout star and unexpected savior; iconic paddock & finale appearances.
Velociraptor dinosaur
Pack of highly intelligent predators stalking survivors through the park.
Dilophosaurus dinosaur
Frilled, venom-spitting ambusher; infamous for the Nedry encounter.
Brachiosaurus dinosaur
Majestic sauropod; first dinosaur revealed on-screen.
Triceratops dinosaur
“Sick Trike” scene; hands-on paleontology and practical effects showcase.
Gallimimus dinosaur
Fast-running herd; stampeded across the plains into T. rex hunting zone.
Parasaurolophus dinosaur
Hadrosaur cameo in river and field shots.
John Hammond human
Wealthy industrialist and founder of Jurassic Park (portrayed by Richard Attenborough).
Dr. Alan Grant human
Paleontologist and protagonist who studies dinosaur behavior (Sam Neill).
Dr. Ellie Sattler human
Paleobotanist and field scientist who assists in studying park dinosaurs (Laura Dern).
Dr. Ian Malcolm human
Mathematician and chaos theorist who warns about the park’s hubris (Jeff Goldblum).
Lex Murphy human
John Hammond's granddaughter; resourceful child who helps reboot systems.
Tim Murphy human
John Hammond's grandson; fascinated by dinosaurs and video games.
Dennis Nedry human
InGen computer programmer who sabotages park systems (ill-fated due to Dilophosaurus).
Robert Muldoon human
Game warden and raptor handler who understands predator behavior.
Donald Gennaro human
Lawyer and corporate representative overseeing the park’s safety evaluation.
Ray Arnold human
Park chief engineer responsible for control systems.
Dr. Henry Wu human
Geneticist who appears as the park's lead scientist (small role in the original film).

Tyrannosaurus rex dinosaur
Family unit (Buck, Doe, infant); rescue and San Diego rampage set pieces.
Velociraptor dinosaur
Territorial pack in the long grass and village; coordinated, agile hunters.

Tiny “Compies” that swarm; surprising danger in numbers on the beach & stream.
Stegosaurus dinosaur
First franchise appearance; defensive tail swings during photo encounter.
Pachycephalosaurus dinosaur
Head-butting “Pachy”; crowd-pleaser during the round‑up sequence.
Parasaurolophus dinosaur
Captured by InGen team; prominent in the herd round‑up.
Gallimimus dinosaur
Further herd behavior glimpses on Isla Sorna.
Dr. Ian Malcolm human
Returns as the protagonist leading a documentary team to Isla Sorna.
Sarah Harding human
Paleontologist and animal behaviorist who studies dinosaurs in the wild.
Roland Tembo human
Big-game hunter hired to capture dinosaurs.
Nick Van Owen human
Documentary filmmaker and activist with field experience on Isla Sorna.
Eddie Carr human
Field equipment specialist and rescue team member.
Peter Ludlow human
InGen executive involved with relocating dinosaurs to the mainland.
Jurassic Park III (2001) — Dinosaurs & People

Main antagonist; river pursuit and plane crash; famously kills a juvenile T. rex.
Velociraptor dinosaur
Male and female packs; advanced communication and nest‑protection behavior.
Pteranodon dinosaur
Aviary escape sequence; aerial predation and nest defense.
Ankylosaurus dinosaur
Heavily armored herbivore; club tail cameo on Sorna.
Corythosaurus dinosaur
Hadrosaur with prominent crest; herd cameo alongside other herbivores.
Ceratosaurus dinosaur
Brief encounter near Spinosaurus scat; retreats on sensing Spino.
Brachiosaurus dinosaur
Return appearance; peaceful browsing establishes Sorna’s ecology.
Dr. Alan Grant human
Returned protagonist; dragged into Isla Sorna rescue mission.
Paul Kirby human
Charismatic businessman who hires Grant to find his missing son.
Amanda Kirby human
Paul’s wife; part of the rescue team.
Eric Kirby human
The missing boy whose disappearance triggers the rescue mission.
Billy Brennan human
Pilot and longtime associate who helps transport the group around Sorna.

Weaponized prototype; night‑stalker with heightened senses and cruelty.

Central to rescue and bond with Owen; pivotal in Indoraptor’s defeat.

Mosasaurus dinosaur

Claire Dearing human

Owen Grady human

Dr. Henry Wu human
Geneticist whose work continues to impact events off-island.
Maisie Lockwood human
Granddaughter of Benjamin Lockwood; her existence ties into genetic ethics subplot.

Corporate figure orchestrating the auction and dinosaur trafficking plot.

Former partner of John Hammond, connected to dinosaur conservation efforts.

Technician who helps Claire and Owen during the rescue and later in the U.S.
Zia Rodriguez human

Ian Malcolm human
